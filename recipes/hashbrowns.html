Hash Browns Recipe

Description

This recipe describes an easy way to make delicious pasta napoletana.

Anyone can make it!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Heat water in a pot
  2. Heat up oil in a pan
  3. Use a grater to slice up potatoes into shreds into the pan
  4. Fry for 7 minutes while flipping the sides of the hash brown every 2 to 3 minutes
