Sunny Side Up Eggs Recipe

Description

This recipe describes an easy way to make delicious pasta napoletana.

Anyone can make it!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Heat up the pan at low simmer
  2. Add 2 tablespoons of butter into the pan
  3. Crack an egg into the pan
  4. Fry for 3 minutes/li>
