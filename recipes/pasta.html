Pasta Napoletana Recipe

Description

This recipe describes an easy way to make delicious pasta napoletana.

Anyone can make it!

Ingredients

  • Penne
  • Mutti cherry tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat water in a pot
  2. Heat up oil in a pan
  3. Cut up tomatoes and cook them in the pan
  4. Add chopped garlic and continue to cook
  5. Cook pasta
  6. Strain out pasta
  7. Mix pasta with cooked tomatoes
  8. Add salt, pepper, and parmesan cheese to the mix
